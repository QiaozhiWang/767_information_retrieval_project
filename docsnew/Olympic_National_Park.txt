(times to hike should typically be doubled.) the coastal strip is more readily accessible than the interior of the olympics; due to the difficult terrain, very few backpackers venture beyond casual day-hiking distances. the most popular piece of the coastal strip is the 9-mile (14 km) ozette loop. the park service runs a registration and reservation program to control usage levels of this area.
