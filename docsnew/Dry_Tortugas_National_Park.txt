classified as a borderline sub-tropical-tropical ecosystem, hosting species that do not breed or are uncommon anywhere else in the continental united states. when a cold front along florida wiped out 96 percent of branching coral in 1976-77, once extensive branching coral formations became rubble fields. 15 15 16 birds edit dry tortugas national park has an official bird list of 299 species. of
