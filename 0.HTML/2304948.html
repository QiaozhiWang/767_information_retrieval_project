Chelsea Lately (TV Series)

Episode #6.118 (2012)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • In her opening monologue, host Chelsea Handler talks about her hate of visiting doctors, but despite that why she got a complete physical in London and what the results of that physical are. Chelsea and her round table panelists Michael Yo, Sarah Colonna and Ryan Stout discuss Rob Kardashian tweeting that he is going to USC law school which prompted USC law school to tweet that they have no record of him even having applied, "Fifty Shades of Grey" author E.L. James issuing a CD of music mentioned in the book as an accompaniment to reading the book, and David Hasselhoff's claim that he was in the running for some of movie's most iconic roles including Indiana Jones. Inspired by the Olympics, Chelsea holds her own athletic competition for her three most athletic staff members. And Chelsea chats with guest Wiz Khalifa about his new CD "O.N.I.F.C.", having a strain of cannabis named after him, being friends with Snoop Dogg, his love of old school music, being engaged to Amber Rose who happens to be Kanye West's old girlfriend, and his close relationship with his father.

    - Written by Huggo
