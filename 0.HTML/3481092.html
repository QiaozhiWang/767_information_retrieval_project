Heavy Meddling (2014)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • During flamboyant buddy Zach's vacation, Andrew enjoyed teaming up with considerate George. When Zach realizes his quirks diminish Andrew's eagerness to be his wingman, his semi-jocular suggestion to try other partners is taken seriously, but although it works professionally for Andrew, both friends are miserable without their mate. A campaign for baby animal suits arouses Simon's hope of grandchildren, from Sydney, whose sexually enjoyable relationship with laid-back neighbor hunk Owen is under increasing stress as she tries to hide and deny the truth.

    - Written by KGF Vissers

  • Simon's desperation for grandchildren forces him to interfere with Sydney's new relationship. Meanwhile, Zach and Andrew try working with other partners.
