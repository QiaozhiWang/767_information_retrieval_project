Bob's Burgers (TV Series)

The Millie-churian Candiate (2015)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • Tina and Louise volunteer to run Jimmy Junior's class president campaign in order to keep Millie (guest voice Molly Shannon) from winning, and ruining the school. Meanwhile, Bob becomes obsessed with a knife after talking himself into buying it for $300.

  • Louise knows politics belong in either the trash or the toilet. Bob buys a $300 knife and goes blade to head with Teddy and his hammer. At Wagstaff, Dancin' Jimmy Pesto Jr. and Chess Club's Henry Haber run a clean campaign for President until Millie Frong takes a break from doing the 4th grade thing. When Millie declares her candidacy with a platform that includes mandatory alphabetic besties, Louise knows her mission. Plans go badly awry... Louise is forced to throw her bunny ears into the ring, figuratively. But, Louise is a pawn in a much deeper game. Beware of the quiet ones, especially when they do not carry a big stick.

    - Written by LA-Lawyer
