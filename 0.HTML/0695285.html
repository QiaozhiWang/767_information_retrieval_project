Saved by the Bell: The New Class (TV Series)

Desperately Seeking Work (1997)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • At the mall, Screech gets a job as a security guard and abuses his authority. Ryan helps Nicky get a job at Media Mania. But Nicky gets angry when Ryan sticks him with all the tasks no one wants to do so Nicky plots to get back at Ryan by tricking him into taking a job he doesn't want. Katie has trouble finding work.She applies for several jobs but Eric and Liz are hired at the gym and cookie store over her, and she doesn't get a job at the Teen Machine. When she hears that Media Mania is looking for a computer bookkeeper, she lies on her application to get the job. But, when she causes the computer to crash, her boss figures out Katie's lie and fires her, leaving her despondent that no one wants her as an employee. Will a motivational speech from Olympic gymnast Shannon Miller help Katie find her way?

    - Written by Chris the Geek

  • At the mall, after Katie lies on a job application and is fired, she feels despondent that no one will hire her. Meanwhile, Ryan and Nicky get jobs at the same store and feud while Screech abuses his authority as a security guard.
