Human Target (TV Series)

Salvage & Reclamation (2010)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • A National Geographic photographer accidentally discovers a lost, stolen treasure. Chance tries to rescue the man who is now the target of a South American army and a deadly bounty hunter. In the process, Chance reunites with a fiery former flame.

    - Written by FOX

  • Chance's team drops him in South American republic San Carlos to rescue English anthropology professor Doug Slocum from ruthless colonel Vasquez. As they flee and wait for the plane Guerero has trouble navigating anyway, Doug shows he found the gold bullion treasure of murdered president Sandolval. It proves irresistible to their military pursuers, private 'salvage and claim' pirate Bertram and even Maria Gallego, once Chance's local lover but also a retired guerrilla brigadier.

    - Written by KGF Vissers
