One-Eyed Jacks (1961)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 3 plot summaries

  • Running from the law after a bank robbery in Mexico, Dad Longworth finds an opportunity to take the stolen gold and leave his partner Rio to be captured. Years later, Rio escapes from the prison where he has been since, and hunts down Dad for revenge. Dad is now a respectable sheriff in California, and has been living in fear of Rio's return.

    - Written by Ken Yousten <kyousten@bev.net>

  • After successfully robbing a bank in Sonora, Mexico Dad Longworth abandons his surviving partner Rio who is caught and imprisoned by the Mexican Federales. Five years later Rio manages to escape and wants his revenge. He soon learns that Dad Longworth is now the Sheriff in Monterey, California where there also happens to be a bank just waiting to be robbed. Longworth has done well for himself - he's just been re-elected Sheriff - is now married and has a lovely step daughter, Louisa. As Louisa and Rio fall in love, killing Longworth becomes more of a problem but the Sheriff makes it all easier when he arrests Rio for robbing the bank - something he did not do.

    - Written by garykmcd

  • After robbing a Mexican bank, Dad Longworth takes the loot and leaves his partner Rio to be captured but Rio escapes and searches for Dad in California.

    - Written by nufs68
