Phineas and Ferb (TV Series)

Sipping with the Enemy/Tri-State Treasure: Boot of Secrets (2012)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • In "Sipping with the Enemy," Dr. Doofenshmirtz' daughter Vanessa runs across Major Monogram's son Monty at a coffee shop, subsequently enjoying an impromptu date. Spotting Perry, Vanessa entreats him to keep their meeting low key, which becomes more difficult when Dr. Doofenshmirtz arrives to suck the cool out of the younger generation. Meanwhile, Phineas decides to put on a magic show after Dad pulls a coin from behind his ear. In "Tri-State Treasure: Boot of Secrets," it's Danville's annual Antique Show and Swap Meet. Dad's fondest wish is to find the rarest of antiques - the one-of-a-kind 1807 Dorcham and Wesley boot scrapper. Discovering a map to this coveted prize, Phineas and Ferb know what they're gonna do this day.

    - Written by statmanjeff
