Troldspejlet Special: Halloween (2001)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • Jakob Stegelmann looks at the phenomenon of Halloween, reviewing the combo DVD release of The Mummy (1999) and The Mummy Returns (2001), and DVD release of Young Frankenstein (1974) and King Kong (1933), the VHS/DVD release of Sleepy Hollow (1999), the DVD compilation Scooby Doo: De første mysterier, collecting the first 5 episodes of "Scooby Doo, Where Are You!" (1969), and the books Gys, gru og græskarspøgelser - idébog til Halloween og andre gyserfester (by Birgit Neiser & Ralf Butschkow), Halloween (by Willy Louise, Stram Prahl & Anna Mette Bladt), Halloween bogen (by Jane Bull), Den ægte Halloween bog (by Katrina Lantos Swett & Annette Tillemann-Dick). Also the video games EA Sports F1 2001 (PC & Playstation 2), Tropico (PC), Lego Racers 2 (PC), Master of Atlantis: Poseidon (PC), and a look at the upcoming video games Crash Bandicoot: The Wrath of Cortex (Playstation 2), Robot Wars: Advanced Destruction and Advance Wars (Game Boy Advance), and tips to the video games XG3: Extreme G Racing (Playstation 2) and Conflict Zone (PC). Also a look at useful video game websites.

    - Written by nathue
