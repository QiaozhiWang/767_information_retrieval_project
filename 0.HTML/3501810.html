Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • Star Wars Episode 38 is a movie completely spoofing the original trilogy of star wars with jokes and slapstick humor in almost every scene. Follows the characters from the Original Trilogy as if they were comedians years after Episode 6. If you like parodies of Star Wars this movie is for you.
