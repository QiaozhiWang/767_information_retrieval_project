King & Maxwell (2013– )

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • Sean King and Michelle Maxwell aren't your typical pair of private investigators. Both are former Secret Service agents, and their unique skill set (not to mention their razor-sharp chemistry) often gives them a leg up on both suspects and conventional law enforcement.

    - Written by Anonymous

  • Sean King and Michelle Maxwell aren't just any private investigators. They're former Secret Service Agents who were disgraced because of their failed assignments. They have a successful firm and start doing work with the FBI despite FBI Agent Rigby's hate for them. While working cases, Sean gets pulled into a conspiracy on his last assignment as a Secret Service Agent that ended his career.
