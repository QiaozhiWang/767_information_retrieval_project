Rocky and His Friends (1959–1964)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • Animated antics of flying squirrel Rocket J. Squirrel, or Rocky, and his dim companion, Bullwinkle J. Moose. And let's not forget the two spies who always complicate things for our heroes: Boris Badenov and Natasha Fatale, who incognito try and help Rocky and Bullwinkle then immediately put them in life-threatening situations that result in major cliffhangers. Also features Grimm Fairy Tales Jay Ward-style and Peabody and Sherman taking intriguing trips through time courtesy of the Way-Back machine.

    - Written by Dylan Self <robocoptng986127@aol.com>

  • An comedic anthology featuring primarily the adventures of a heroic flying squirrel and his dumb moose friend.

    - Written by Kenneth Chisholm
