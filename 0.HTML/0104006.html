Consenting Adults (1992)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • Richard and Priscilla Parker's lives take a turn for the better when Eddy and Kay move into the house next door. Eddy's a risk taker and shows his new neighbours how to enjoy life at the expense of a rule or convention or two. What Richard doesn't realise is that Eddy's little games are just a prelude to something that's intended to destroy his neighbours' lives.

    - Written by Rob Hartill

  • The Parkers are a normal couple, which meet the Otis, their new neighbours. They enjoy life, travel a lot, earn and spend money... Their friendship becomes bigger, and spend a lot of time together. The two men and the two women get along well with each other, even too well. Eddy proposes Richard a couple exchange, just for one night. He doesn't think it's right, but Eddy is kind of obsessioned with it. Something will happen that will put Richard in prison.

    - Written by <oscar.esteban@p4.virtual.encomix.com>
