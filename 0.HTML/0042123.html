Lux Video Theatre (1950–1959)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • Live dramatic shows featuring Hollywood stars. Initially, the show was a 30-minute weekly show but when it moved to NBC in August 1954, the show was extended to 60-minutes and the plays were primarily adaptations of motion pictures and a host was added. The host would introduce each act and would conduct an interview with the stars at the end of the play.

    - Written by Jack McKillop
