Modest Mouse: King Rat (2009 Video)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • The music video of the song King Rat by Modest Mouse. Heath Ledger "fully conceived [it] down to the last detail" but it was unfinished at the time of his death. The LA art collective 'The Masses' helped complete the video. The video was designed "to raise awareness on modern whaling practices." The video contains fairly graphic animated scenes, but it delivers and hopefully cements the message in people's minds.
