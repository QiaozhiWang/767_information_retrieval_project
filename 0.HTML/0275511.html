Medal of Honor: Allied Assault (2002 Video Game)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • In this game, you play a soldier who participates in several missions during the Second World War. As a soldier, you fight in the frontline with D-Day, June 6th, 1944 at Omaha Beach being a particular highlight of your career. Later, you become an secret agent who must infilitrate The Third Reiche in order to undermine the Axis any way you can.

    - Written by Kenneth Chisholm (kchishol@rogers.com)

  • You play a soldier, then spy, who performs various perilious missions during World War II.
