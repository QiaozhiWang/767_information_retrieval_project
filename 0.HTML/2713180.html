Fury (2014)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 3 plot summaries

  • April, 1945. As the Allies make their final push in the European Theatre, a battle-hardened Army sergeant named Wardaddy (Brad Pitt) commands a Sherman tank and his five-man crew on a deadly mission behind enemy lines. Outnumbered, and out-gunned, Wardaddy and his men face overwhelming odds in their heroic attempts to strike at the heart of Nazi Germany.

    - Written by Sony Pictures Entertainment

  • In 1945, in the World War II in Germany, the tough Sergeant Don 'Wardaddy' Collier commands a tank and survives to a German attack with his veteran crew composed of Boyd 'Bible' Swan, Trini 'Gordo' Garcia and Grady 'Coon-Ass' Travis. He receives the rookie soldier Norman Ellison as the substitute for his deceased gunner and he tries to harden the youth along the battles.

    - Written by Claudio Carvalho, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
