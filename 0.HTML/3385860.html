A Belly Full of Anger (2012)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • Even the most jaded among you will be disgusted by the sight of it! Can you stand the frankness of truth behind every scene? Deranged killers who use an acrobatic form of boxing called 'kung fu' - their art is murder, their tools are their hands and feet, the canvas is anyone in their way... beyond the dreams of the most brutal butcher... an army of men ripped to pieces by justice... the law of the jungle is shown in all its savage horror through the use of techniques of terrible bloodflow... John Yohan Chung and Don Nguyen lead a cast of promising young martial artists. This special English edition is ladled with cast members of MST3K, Mr. Show, and The Firesign Theatre providing voice overs in a desperate attempt to give meaning to the director's increasingly troubled life.

    - Written by Anonymous
