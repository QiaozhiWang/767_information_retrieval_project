MacGyver (TV Series)

Deadly Silents (1991)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • Pinky, a star of silent films, is showing his films to MacGyver. Pinky hopes that The Phoenix Foundation will preserve his films. Pinky and his friend Karl were also good at building sets and making special effects. There was however, a problem: they were both in love with the same girl and became enemies. Two masked men invade Pinky's house and take his films. Can MacGyver retrieve them?

    - Written by Toni Tapola, Finland

  • While MacGyver visits black and white age movie star Pinky Burnette, who shows off just recovered recordings, robbers seize the lot, using old-fashioned special effects. Pinky recognizes those as the work of his former comrade and now enemy Karl, whose assistant Neil acts as henchman. Pinky chases them, so mac comes along as chaperon, which proves a hard and dangerous task, as Karl laid a whole course of traps in a derelict film studio.

    - Written by KGF Vissers
