Chelsea Lately (TV Series)

Episode #7.86 (2013)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • In her opening monologue, host Chelsea Handler talks about why she may change her mind about not getting married. Chelsea and her round table panelists John Caparulo, Dan Finnerty and Gary Valentine discuss Lindsay Lohan requesting to be released from her court ordered rehab early for her birthday, John Travolta crashing the wedding of a couple he met the previous evening, an upstate New York mother of three being busted for heading a marijuana ring worth $3 million, and a postal carrier claiming disability being charged with fraud after she was seen doing many of the things she claimed she couldn't do on The Price Is Right (1972). And Chelsea chats with guest LeAnn Rimes about her new CD "Spitfire" many of the songs on which are inspired by her husband Eddie Cibrian, she and Eddie contemplating doing a television show together, and going into rehab for stress.

    - Written by Huggo
