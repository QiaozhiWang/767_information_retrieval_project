Switch (1997–2000)

Plot Summary

    Showing one plot summary

  • The series "Switch" was produced in the years 1996-1999 by "Visuelle" in Köln Ossendorf and broadcasted in the years 1997-2000. The cast spoofs the German TV series and a lot of international music artists (The Bloodhound Gang, Robbie Williams, Tom Jones, etc.). There was also an invention of the writers. At "Fakten Fakten Fakten" the whole cast sat on a round and made pointless games. Hoëcker didn't understand the games or he told something about Lemgo. So someone said: "Hoëcker, Sie sind raus!!" (Hoëcker, you're out!).

    - Written by Stefanie Schmid
