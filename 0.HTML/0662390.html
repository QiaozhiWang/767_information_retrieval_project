Northern Exposure (TV Series)

Sex, Lies and Ed's Tapes (1990)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • There's much to deal with in Cicely when Shelly's husband arrives with the hopes of taking her back to Saskatchewan, much to the astonishment of Holling. Furthermore, Maggie's boyfriend fears he will soon die as the next victim of the O'Connell curse and Ed searches for inspiration while working on his movie script.

    - Written by Skeeter700

  • Holling learns his waitress and soon-to-be fiancée, Shelly, is married already. Her Canadian husband, ice hockey-player Wayne Jones (20), turns up, eager for a divorce so he can wed another - or make their own marriage work. She has a hysterical 'pregnancy'. Joel finds a probably benign melanoma on Maggie's friend, Rick Pederson, who fears he will become the next of her dying spouses. Joel encourages Ed to keep working on his Hollywood script, taking inspiration from real life rather then blockbusters. Female love proves even scarcer then previously appeared.

    - Written by KGF Vissers
