Married to the Blob (2014)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 2 plot summaries

  • The Android's Dungeon owner falls for Kumiko, a Japanese woman who shares his interest in comics and, advised by the Simpsons, he starts to date her. Then her father arrives from Japan and insists she return home with him. Homer takes him out for a night on the town where, after consuming large quantities of the hallucination-inducing snake rice wine, the father comes to perceive reality through fantasy and permits the couple to marry.

    - Written by don @ minifie-1

  • Comic Book Guy seeks romantic advice from Homer when he falls for a Japanese woman who's interested in manga.
