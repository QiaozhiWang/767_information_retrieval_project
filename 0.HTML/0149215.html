The Swan Princess: The Mystery of the Enchanted Treasure (1998)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 3 plot summaries

  • The royal couple Odette and Derek face yet another evil magician, this time a woman named Zelda. Lusting for the treasure of the Forbidden Arts, which will give her absolute power, Zelda kidnaps Odette as ransom. Derek and several animal friends head off to rescue Odette.

    - Written by Anonymous

  • Now the royal couple Odette and Derek face yet another evil magician, this time a woman named Zelda. She was once a consort to Rothbart, plans to wreak havoc to their kingdom by stealing the Forbidden Arts. As part of her plan, she captures a Yakey-bird named Whizzer who has the ability to imitate any voice after hearing it only once. Threatening his life, she sends Whizzer to the castle at Swan Lake as a spy. There, Whizzer overhears that Derek did not destroy Rothbart's notes on the Forbidden Arts. The Prince then must face Zelda in an exciting showdown to rescue Odette.

    - Written by Anthony Pereyra {hypersonic91@yahoo.com}

  • Zelda, an evil sorceress, plans to wreak havoc on the peaceful land of Swan Lake by stealing the Forbidden Arts - a magical treasure that gives its keeper unlimited power. As part of her evil plan, Zelda kidnaps Odette, the Swan Princess and demands that Prince Derek turn over the Forbidden Arts in exchange for Odette's safety. The Prince then must face Zelda in an exciting showdown to rescue the Swan Princess.

    - Written by James Drury <jaguar12sprint.ca>
