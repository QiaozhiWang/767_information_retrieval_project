go to war. when world war i ends, elizabeth receives a telegram that john has been killed in action. she finds comfort in larry and they marry. john returns 20 years later, disfigured, with a new identity, erik, and an adopted daughter, margaret. john/erik and elizabeth accidentally meet and he learns that he has a son, drew. john must then decide whether or not to reveal his true identity.
