Dhadkan (2000)

Plot Summary

    Showing all 4 plot summaries

  • Wealthy Anjali falls in love with poor Dev, both want to get married, but Anjali's family objects and instead get her married to equally wealthy Ram, who lives with his uncaring step-mother and siblings. Anjali finds out that Ram is not respected, but does not care as she does not love him. Ram continues to be affectionate toward her and his family, but Anjali cannot get Dev out of her mind. Then things change, when Ram decides to assert himself and asks his family to leave him and his wife alone. Anjali starts warming up to Ram, when Dev shows up - this Dev is not the poor Dev that Anjali once knew, this Dev is a multi-millionaire, scheming, and devious, and a Dev who will try to win Anjali over by hook or by crook.

    - Written by Calista

  • Dev and Anjali are in love . Dev comes from a poor background while Anjali is a rich man's daughter . Anjali's father disapproves of Dev and arranges her marriage with Ram . Ram is an affectionate husband but Anjali is still haunted by memories of her past love and does not respond to the love of Ram . Ram lives with his stepfamily who hate him , but Ram believes that one day they will surely undergo a change of heart . But that does not happen and hence Ram asks his stepfamily to leave the home . Anjali starts loving Ram , but Dev returns in her life . He has become a millionaire and wants to win the love of Anjali . Therefore he creates a financial crisis in Ram's life . Anjali is now torn between two men who love her equally .

    - Written by Soumitra

  • Wealthy Anjali (Shilpa Shetty), is passionately in love with poor Dev (Sunil Shetty), both plans to get married. But Anjali's family objects and instead get her married to equally wealthy Ram (Akshay Kumar), who lives with his uncaring step-mother and siblings. But Ram believes that one day his family will surely undergo a change of heart. However, Anjali finds out that Ram is not respected, but does not care as she does not love him, because she is still haunted by memories of her past love. Ram continues to be affectionate towards her and his family, then things change, when Ram decides to assert himself and asks his family to leave him and his wife alone. Anjali starts warming up to Ram, when Dev shows up - this Dev is not the poor Dev that Anjali once knew, this Dev is a multi-millionaire, scheming, and devious, and a Dev who will try to win Anjali over by hook or by crook. But Anjali bypass her haunted memories of Dev and finally falls in love with Ram and will not leave him by any chance.

    - Written by gavin@sunny_deol2009@yahoo.com

  • Anjali lives a rich lifestyle along with her businessman dad and housewife mom. Her dad would like her to get married to Ram, who comes from a wealthy family, and approves of Anjali. But she has given her heart to a poor young man, Dev, who has no family background and has lived the life of an orphan. Anjali is coerced into marrying Ram, who is sensitive to her emotions and gives her plenty of time and space. Then their lives are turned upside down when Dev re-enters their lives, this time as the son of a wealthy woman. When he finds out that Anjali has married Ram, he conceives a plot to financially ruin Ram and thus make him ineligible for Anjali. The question remains, will Anjali support Ram or re-kindle her romance with Dev?

    - Written by rAjOo (gunwanti@hotmail.com)
