and is not of this earth. as a young man, he journeys to discover where he came from and what he was sent here to do. but the hero in him must emerge if he is to save the world from annihilation and become the symbol of hope for all mankind.        written by  warner bros. pictures kal-el, son of jor-el and lara is sent to earth after his home planet krypton leads to a complete incineration. now taking the name "clark kent", he then discovers his true persona when he is guided to become superman: a hero committed to protect earth's fate and the harm that threatens it. however, general zod, a citizen of krypton and its military leader looks at earth's fate differently and decides to use it with a sacrifice for all humans. superman, with the help of present military and news reporter lois lane makes an alliance together to stop zod from obliterating human existence.        a young clark kent, isolated and scared discovers unusual powers. although his adoptive planet is gone, survivors
