excessively awkward boy from an anatolian village starts off to istanbul to inherit all the properties. however, during journey to istanbul to own all the riches and properties sakir gets into trouble with gardrop fuat who is horrendously irritable, comes newly out of prison, moreover gardrop is also concerned with a relative of sakir, called haci who is not willing to share inheritance goes all out to eliminate sakir and possess all the wealth. hereby, in istanbul sakir is forced to struggle against haci and his collaborationist gardrop who chases sakir within an intense hunt. sooner or later, sakir puts out of sight his opponents; nevertheless being awkward never leaves him in peace and sakir burns out half of istanbul with a single match and returns to village.        written by
