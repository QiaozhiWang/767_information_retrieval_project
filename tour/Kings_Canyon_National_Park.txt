national parks campgrounds are located in oak woodlands in the warm, dry foothills and in the higher, cooler conifer forests. they range in elevation from 2,100 to 7,500 feet (640 to 2,290 m). lodgepole, dorst, grant grove and atwell mill campgrounds are near giant sequoia groves. in general, higher elevation campgrounds are cooler and closer to giant sequoias. see also edit national
