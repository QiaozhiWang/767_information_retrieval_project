at the turn of the century in 1901. three men are on a mission from the ira to steal all the gold in the vaults of the bank of england. norgate, their leader, discovers the bank's weak spot: an old forgotten sewer straight under the vaults. but they have to deal with captain fitch, a young but suspicious bank guard.        written by  mattias thuresson early in the last century. irish rebels descend on london intent on emptying the gold vaults of the bank of england. they are assisted by an american sympathizer, charles norgate, an engineer by profession who is tasked on finding a weak spot in the bank's security. he befriends the captain of the guards unit that provide overnight security and gains access to the subterranean passages under the bank. he finds the fatal flaw in their security - an abandoned sewer line that runs very near the gold vaults - and it's a race against time to break in.        written by  garykmcd synopsis this crime caper based on a legendary robbery
