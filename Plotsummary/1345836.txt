          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Despite his tarnished reputation after the events of The Dark Knight, in which he took the rap for Dent's crimes, Batman feels compelled to intervene to assist the city and its police force which is struggling to cope with Bane's plans to destroy the city.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=WellardRockard&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>WellardRockard</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
8 years after the events of The Dark Knight find Gotham at a time of peace. This is due to Batman taking the fall for Harvey Dent's murder. However, a new evil force named Bane has arrived in Gotham and aims to take over the city and expose the truth behind who Harvey Dent really was. Now that Wayne Manor has been completely rebuilt - Bruce Wayne has become almost reclusive, rarely leaving the estate. And with Bane taking over the city by force, it forces Batman to come out of retirement. But his allies are few and far apart. An elusive jewel thief by the name of Selina Kyle could be the key to stopping Bane - but whose side is she on?              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=halo1k&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>halo1k</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
8 years after the Batman taking the fall for murders committed by Harvey, Gotham has a new enemy - Bane, a former member of the League of Shadows. Earlier, with Wayne Manor completely rebuilt, Bruce had become completely reclusive, rarely leaving the estate. Bane plans to continue the work of Ra's Al Ghul by destroying Gotham. With allies being few and far away, Batman teams up with elusive jewel thief Selina Kyle,who may also be the key to stopping Bane. But whose side is she really on?              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=srijanarora-152-448595&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>srijanarora-152-448595</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
Eight years after the Joker's reign of anarchy, the Dark Knight, with the help of the enigmatic Selina, is forced from his imposed exile to save Gotham City, now on the edge of total annihilation, from the brutal guerrilla terrorist Bane.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Paulrichard&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Paulrichard</a></span></em>      </li>
