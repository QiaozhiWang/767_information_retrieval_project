  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 4 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Death Row guards at a penitentiary, in the 1930's, have a moral dilemma with their job when they discover one of their prisoners, a convicted murderer, has a special gift.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Guy%20Johns&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>Guy Johns</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Paul Edgecomb is a slightly cynical veteran prison guard on Death row in the 1930's. His faith, and sanity, deteriorated by watching men live and die, Edgecomb is about to have a complete turn around in attitude. Enter John Coffey, He's eight feet tall. He has hands the size of waffle irons. He's been accused of the murder of two children... and he's afraid to sleep in a cell without a night-light. And Edgecomb, as well as the other prison guards - Brutus, a sympathetic guard, and Percy, a stuck up, perverse, and violent person, are in for a strange experience that involves intelligent mice, brutal executions, and the revelation about Coffey's innocence and his true identity.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Kadi%20Lynnith&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>Kadi Lynnith</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
It's just another normal day on the Green Mile for prison guard Paul Edgecomb. That is until huge John Coffey is sent there. Unlike the hulking brute that Coffey looks like, he is in fact kind at heart. Whilst watching over Coffey, Edgecomb learns that there is more to Coffey than can be seen.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=FilmFanUk&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>FilmFanUk</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
The lives of guards on Death Row are affected by one of their charges: a black man accused of child murder and rape, yet who has a mysterious gift.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Chris%20White&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Chris White</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
