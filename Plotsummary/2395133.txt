          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Adjust Your Tracking is a feature-length documentary film directed by Levi "Dabeedo" Peretic and Dan Kinem. It's a passion project made by true lovers of the format hoping to capture why VHS holds such a special place in so many different people's hearts. The film features interviews with VHS collectors, video store owners, filmmakers, and distributors.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Dan%20Kinem&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
