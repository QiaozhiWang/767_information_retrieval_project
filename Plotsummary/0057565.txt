  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 3 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
An executive mortgages all he owns to stage a coup and gain control of the National Shoe Company, with the intent of keeping the company out of the hands of incompetent and greedy executives. He needs the same money, though, to pay the ransom that will possibly save a child's life. His resolution of that dilemma -- the certain loss of the company vs. the probable loss of the child -- makes for one distinct drama, and an ensuing elaborate police procedure makes for a second.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=levin%20%3Clevin%40world.std.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>levin &lt;levin@world.std.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
At a crucial point in his business life, executive Gondo learns that his son has been kidnapped and that the ransom demanded is near the amount Gondo has raised for a critical business deal. Gondo is prepared to pay the ransom - that is, until he learns that the kidnappers have mistakenly abducted not Gondo's son, but the child of Gondo's chauffeur. Now Gondo must decide whether the other man's child is equally worth saving.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Jim%20Beaver%20%3Cjumblejim%40prodigy.net%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>Jim Beaver &lt;jumblejim@prodigy.net&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="odd">
        <p class="plotSummary">
An executive of a shoe company becomes a victim of extortion when his chauffeur's son is kidnapped and held for ransom.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=duke1029%40aol.com&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>duke1029@aol.com</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
