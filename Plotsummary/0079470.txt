<a href="/title/tt0079470/?ref_=ttpl_pl_tt"
itemprop='url'>Life of Brian</a>         <span class="nobr">
             (1979)
         </span>
    </h3>
  </div>

  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 5 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
The story of Brian of Nazareth, born on the same day as Jesus of Nazareth, who takes a different path in life that leads to the same conclusion. Brian joins a political resistance movement aiming to get the Romans out of Judea. Brian scores a victory of sorts when he manages to paint political slogans on an entire wall in the city of Jerusalem. The movement is not very effective but somehow Brian becomes a prophet and gathers his own following. His fate is sealed however and he lives a very short life.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=garykmcd&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>garykmcd</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Irreverent satire of Biblical films and religious intolerance focuses on Brian, a Jew in Roman-occupied Judea. After joining up with an anti-Roman political organization, Brian is mistaken for a prophet, and becomes a reluctant Messiah.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Scott%20Renshaw%20%3Cas.idc%40forsythe.stanford.edu%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>Scott Renshaw &lt;as.idc@forsythe.stanford.edu&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Brian is born in a stable on Christmas, right next to You Know Who. The wise men appear and begin to distribute gifts. The star moves further, so they take it all back and move on. This is how Brian's life goes. The Jews are looking for a release from the Romans, Spiritual and political decay, keep looking for signs and a group decides Brian is the Messiah. He cannot convince them he is not. He joins the Peoples' Front of Judea, one of several dozen separatist groups who actually do nothing, but really hate the Romans. While not about Jesus, it is about those who hadn't time, or interest to listen to his message. Many Political and Social comments.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=John%20Vogel%20%3Cjlvogel%40comcast.net%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>John Vogel &lt;jlvogel@comcast.net&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
The Monty Pyton team tells the life, death and resurrection (?) of Brian from Nazareth, an unwilling prophet who wants to free his land from the oppression of Rome. When Brian covers a huge wall with the writing "ROMANI ITE DOMUM" (Romans go home), a local revolutionary group begins to take him seriously...              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Flavio%20Rizzardi%20%3Cspillo%40maya.dei.unpid.it%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Flavio Rizzardi &lt;spillo@maya.dei.unpid.it&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="odd">
        <p class="plotSummary">
Brian is born on the original Christmas, in the stable next door. He spends his life being mistaken for a messiah.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Jon%20Reeves%20%3Cjreeves%40imdb.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_5"
>Jon Reeves &lt;jreeves@imdb.com&gt;</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>

Brian Cohen is born in a stable a few doors down from the one in which Jesus is born, a fact which initially confuses the three wise men who come to praise the baby Jesus...    </p>
