small-town america for a teaching position. he allows himself to be swept into a relationship with charlotte haze, his widowed and sexually famished landlady, whom he marries in order that he might pursue the woman's 14-year-old flirtatious daughter, lolita, with whom he has fallen hopelessly in love, but whose affections shall be thwarted by a devious trickster named clare quilty.
