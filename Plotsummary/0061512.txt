<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 4 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Luke Jackson is a cool, gutsy prisoner in a Southern chain gang, who, while refusing to buckle under to authority, keeps escaping and being recaptured. The prisoners admire Luke because, as Dragline explains it, "You're an original, that's what you are!" Nevertheless, the camp staff actively works to crush Luke until he finally breaks.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=alfiehitchie&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>alfiehitchie</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Lucas Jackson, natural born world shaker, someone with more guts than brains, a man who refuses to conform to the rules he has been given. Sent to a prison camp for a misdemeanor Luke soon gains respect and becomes an idol. He has some fun in jail doing things for the hell of it, after his mother dies the Bosses put him in the box afraid he might want to attend the funeral. When he gets out he runs and gets caught and runs and gets caught, the bosses try to break him but he just won't break.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Sandra%20Spence&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>Sandra Spence</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Luke is sent to a prison camp, where he gets a reputation as a hard man. The head of the gang hates him, and tries to break him by beating him up. It doesn't work, and he gains respect. His mother dies, and he escapes, but is caught, escapes again, and is caught again. Will the camp bosses ever break him ?              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Colin%20Tinto%20%3Ccst%40imdb.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>Colin Tinto &lt;cst@imdb.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
A man refuses to conform to life in a rural prison.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
