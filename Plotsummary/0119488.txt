  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 4 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
1950's Los Angeles is the seedy backdrop for this intricate noir-ish tale of police corruption and Hollywood sleaze. Three very different cops are all after the truth, each in their own style: Ed Exley, the golden boy of the police force, willing to do almost anything to get ahead, except sell out; Bud White, ready to break the rules to seek justice, but barely able to keep his raging violence under control; and Jack Vincennes, always looking for celebrity and a quick buck until his conscience drives him to join Exley and White down the one-way path to find the truth behind the dark world of L.A. crime.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Greg%20Bole%20%3Cbole%40life.bio.sunysb.edu%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>Greg Bole &lt;bole@life.bio.sunysb.edu&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
LA in the 50's: someone's killing imprisoned mob boss Mickey Cohen's gang. The police, led by Captain Dudley, convince wiseguys from Jersey, Cleveland, and elsewhere to go home. Rich developer, Pierce Patchett, runs a stable of high-class hookers who are ringers for movie stars. The plot to replace Cohen blindsides three plainclothes cops: White watched his father beat his mother to death then vanish, he punishes abusers with quick violence; Exley's father was a hero cop killed mysteriously, he seeks justice by the book; Vicennes, a clothes horse, consults for a Dragnet-like TV show. Will they escape corruption and murder, will they find their own morality?              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=%3Cjhailey%40hotmail.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>&lt;jhailey@hotmail.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Three detectives in the corrupt and brutal L.A. police force of the 1950s use differing methods to uncover a conspiracy behind the shotgun slayings of the patrons at an all-night diner in this lush tribute to tough film noir crime films. Based on the multi-layered James Ellroy novel.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Keith%20Loh%20%3Ckeith%40mwg.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>Keith Loh &lt;keith@mwg.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
As corruption grows in 1950s LA, three policemen - one strait-laced, one brutal, and one sleazy - investigate a series of murders with their own brand of justice.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Trance&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Trance</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
