  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 5 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Richard Hannay is a Canadian visitor to London. At the end of "Mr Memory"'s show in a music hall, he meets Annabella Smith who is running away from secret agents. He accepts to hide her in his flat, but in the night she is murdered. Fearing he could be accused on the girl's murder, Hannay goes on the run to break the spy ring.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Claudio%20Sandrini%20%3Cpulp99%40geocities.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>Claudio Sandrini &lt;pulp99@geocities.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
After a performance by Mr. Memory at a London music hall leads to a brawl, Richard Hannay goes offers to help a mysterious woman, Annabella. She claims she is on the run from foreign spies and he agrees to let her spend the night in his flat. Annabella tells him she'll be off to Scotland in the morning but she is murdered in his flat. Hannay heads to Scotland having only her map and a cryptic reference to the 39 steps. He manages to stay ahead of the police, eventually making his way to Annabelle's contact in Scotland, Professor Jordan. Air Ministry secrets have been stolen but Hannay can't seem to convince anyone. Eventually arrested, he and his new companion Pamela escape and return to London where the solution to the mystery lies.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=garykmcd&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>garykmcd</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Richard Hannay is a Canadian visitor to 1930's London. After a disturbance at a music hall, he meets Annabella Smith who is on the run from foreign agents. He takes her back to his apartment, but they are followed and later that night Annabella is murdered. Hannay goes on the run to break the spy ring and thus prove his innocence.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Col%20Needham%20%3Ccol%40imdb.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>Col Needham &lt;col@imdb.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Richard Hannay is on the run from the police, who suspect him of murdering the woman found stabbed to death in his London flat. This story, including his photograph, are in many of the newspapers throughout Great Britain. In reality, the woman picked Hannay up at the Mr. Memory show at which they both attended, she telling him that there were two men there who were trying to kill her in an effort to obtain information which would breach national security. Hannay is not only on the run from police but is also trying to find out the nature of the potential national security breach in an effort to clear his name. All he knows from the dead woman is that it has something to do with a man in a small town in Scotland, another man who has part of his pinky finger missing and something called the 39 steps. As Hannay is on his journey of discovery all the while trying to elude both the police and the real murderers, he is forced out of circumstances outside of his control to take along a feisty young woman named Pamela whose continued belligerence toward him is despite her precarious situation.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Huggo&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Huggo</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="odd">
        <p class="plotSummary">
A man in London tries to help a counterespionage agent. But when the agent is killed and the man stands accused, he must go on the run to both save himself and also stop a spy ring which is trying to steal top secret information.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=jgp3553%40yahoo.com&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_5"
>jgp3553@yahoo.com</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
