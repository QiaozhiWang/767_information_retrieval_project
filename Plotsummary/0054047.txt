  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 4 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
A bandit terrorizes a small Mexican farming village each year. Several of the village elders send three of the farmers into the United States to search for gunmen to defend them. They end up with seven, each of whom comes for a different reason. They must prepare the town to repulse an army of forty bandits who will arrive wanting food.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=John%20Vogel%20%3Cjlvogel%40comcast.net%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>John Vogel &lt;jlvogel@comcast.net&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
When their small village is once again attacked by the bandit Calvera, three Mexican farmers head north to try and buy guns. They ask Chris Adams to help them but he suggests they hire experienced gunmen instead. He soon recruits five others and eventually accept a sixth who he had initially rejected but made it impossible for him to say no. Together, they develop a plan to secure the town and defend it against Calvera's attack. In that time, friendships are formed and love blossoms between one of the men and a beautiful local girl. Not all of them will survive the attack.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=garykmcd&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>garykmcd</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
A remake of "The Seven Samurai." Seven men are picked to guard a Mexican village from Banditos that come every now and then to take whatever the town has grown since their last visit. When they are hired, they go to the town and teach the villagers how to defend themselves. When the leader of the bandits come, they fight him and his men off. the second time he comes the villagers give the seven to them, due to a heated argument. The leader of the bandits take their guns and throw them out of town he gives them horses and gives their guns back to them when they are far out of town. The seven decide that they aren't going to run, and head back to the village for a final showdown.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Chase%20Ard%20%3CBullitt357%40aol.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>Chase Ard &lt;Bullitt357@aol.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
An oppressed Mexican peasant village assembles seven gunfighters to help defend their homes.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Kenneth%20Chisholm&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Kenneth Chisholm</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
