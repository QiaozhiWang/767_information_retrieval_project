jefferson smith, leader of the boy rangers, is appointed on a lark by the spineless governor of his state. he is reunited with the state's senior senator--presidential hopeful and childhood hero, senator joseph paine. in washington, however, smith discovers many of the shortcomings of the political process as his earnest goal of a national boys' camp leads to a conflict with the state political boss, jim taylor. taylor first tries to corrupt smith and then later attempts to destroy smith through a scandal.        written by  james yu  jamestyu@ccwf.cc.utexas.edu a naive man is appointed to fill a vacancy in the us senate. his plans promptly collide with political corruption, but he doesn't back down.     written by  anonymous synopsis the governor of an unnamed western
