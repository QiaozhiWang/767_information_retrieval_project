  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 4 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
<a href="/name/nm1463454">Kermit the Frog</a> is persuaded by agent <a href="/name/nm0001123">Dom DeLuise</a> to pursue a career in Hollywood. Along the way, Kermit picks up <a href="/name/nm2181680">Fozzie Bear</a>, <a href="/name/nm1465442">Miss Piggy</a>, 'The Great Gonzo', and a motley crew of other Muppets with similar aspirations. Meanwhile, Kermit must elude the grasp of a frog-leg restaurant magnate.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Ray%20Hamel%20%3Chamel%40primate.wisc.edu%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>Ray Hamel &lt;hamel@primate.wisc.edu&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Story of a frog, a bear and a pig (portraying themselves are <a href="/name/nm1463454">Kermit the Frog</a>, <a href="/name/nm2181680">Fozzie Bear</a> and <a href="/name/nm1465442">Miss Piggy</a>) on the road to Hollywood. Kermit claims it is "approximately" the way the Muppets began. While on their nationwide trek, they encounter Doc Hopper who plans to make Kermit his spokesman for his Frog Leg restaurant chain. Muppet characters' first outing in the movies features cameos in the double digits and safe family fun.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Tim%20Kretschmann%20%3CTim.K%40VirComm.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>Tim Kretschmann &lt;Tim.K@VirComm.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
<a href="/name/nm1463454">Kermit the Frog</a> wants fame. Persuaded by an agent to pursue acting, he hops west towards Hollywood! Tagging along: <a href="/name/nm2181680">Fozzie Bear</a>, <a href="/name/nm1465442">Miss Piggy</a>, 'The Great Gonzo', and a motley group of Muppets help dodge evil Doc Hopper and a Cameo-filled cast on the trek to Tinseltown!              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=JK2%20%3CFlickwritr%40aol.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>JK2 &lt;Flickwritr@aol.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
Kermit and his new found friends trek across America to find success in Hollywood, but a frog-legs merchant is after Kermit.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Kenneth%20Chisholm&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Kenneth Chisholm</a></span></em>      </li>
