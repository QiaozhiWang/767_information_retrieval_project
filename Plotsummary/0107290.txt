<a href="/title/tt0107290/?ref_=ttpl_pl_tt"
itemprop='url'>Jurassic Park</a>         <span class="nobr">
             (1993)
         </span>
    </h3>
  </div>

  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 6 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Huge advancements in scientific technology have enabled a mogul to create an island full of living dinosaurs. John Hammond has invited four individuals, along with his two grandchildren, to join him at Jurassic Park. But will everything go according to plan? A park employee attempts to steal dinosaur embryos, critical security systems are shut down and it now becomes a race for survival with dinosaurs roaming freely over the island.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Film_Fan&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>Film_Fan</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
On Isla Nublar, a new park has just been built with genetically engineered dinosaurs. Tragedy strikes when one of the workers is killed by a velociraptor. The founder of the park, John Hammond, (Richard Attenborough) requests Paleontologist Dr. Alan Grant (Sam Neil) and his assistant, Dr. Ellie Sattler (Laura Dern) to come to the park and ensure that it is safe. Also joining them are Hammond's lawyer Donald Gennaro (Martin Ferrero) and chaos theorist Dr. Ian Malcolm (Jeff Goldblum). When they reach the island, they are amazed to discover that Hammond has created living dinosaurs. However, at the same time they all have their doubts. Later, Hammond's grandchildren Lex and Tim (Ariana Richards and Joseph Mazzello) join the group in a tour of the park. Sattler leaves the tour to take care of an ill triceratops. Soon the power in the park is shut down by computer systems geek Dennis Nedry (Wayne Knight) who wishes to steal embryos from the park to sell to a secret buyer. In the process, many dinosaurs escape their paddocks, including the deadly Tyrannosaurus Rex, who, during a thunderstorm, escapes his paddock & attacks the children, and eats Gennaro. Malcolm is injured & Grant and the children are then lost in the park. Meanwhile, Hammond, Sattler and the rest of the operations team learn that Nedry (who in the meantime has been killed) has locked up the computer system to cover his tracks. They attempt to get power back in the park in order to escape the island. After shutting down the system, then restoring it, the group realizes that velociraptors are also on the loose, & are now on the hunt for the visitors.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Anonymous&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>Anonymous</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
On the remote island of Isla Nubar, Scientists are using genetics enhanced with frog DNA, create cloned dinosaurs. While giving a preview tour, the power goes off in the rain and leaves a group of scientists and two kids to survive an island with meat eating dinosaurs.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Kyle%20Dailor&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>Kyle Dailor</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Scientists develop a means of bringing dinosaurs to life using DNA taken from dino blood, which has been preserved inside insects encased in amber. Whilst Hammond is showing off his dinosaur 'theme park' to a selected audience [a lawyer (Gerrano), mathematician (Malcolm), dino expert (Grant), paleobotanist (Sattler) and his grandchildren (Tim & Lex)], Nedry (computer expert) disables the security system so that he can make his escape with some stolen embryos. This enables all the dinosaurs to escape their enclosures...              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Rob%20Hartill&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>Rob Hartill</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
On a remote island, a wealthy entrepreneur secretly creates a theme park featuring living dinosaurs drawn from prehistoric DNA. Before opening the attraction to the public, he invites a top paleontologist, a paleobotanist, a mathematician/theorist, and his two eager grandchildren to experience the park -- and help calm anxious investors. However, their park visit is anything but tranquil as the park's security system breaks down, the prehistoric creatures break out, and the excitement builds to surprising results.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Robert%20Lynch%20%3Cdocrlynch%40yahoo.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_5"
>Robert Lynch &lt;docrlynch@yahoo.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="even">
        <p class="plotSummary">
During a preview tour, a theme park suffers a major power breakdown that allows its cloned dinosaur exhibits to run amok.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Chris%20White&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_6"
>Chris White</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
