  <h1 class="header">Plot Summary</h1>
</div>
    <div class="header">
  <ul class="zebraList">
<div class="header"><div class="nav"><div class="desc">Showing all 5 plot summaries</div></div></div>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
A visiting actress in Washington, D.C., notices dramatic and dangerous changes in the behavior and physical make-up of her 12-year-old daughter. Meanwhile, a young priest at nearby Georgetown University begins to doubt his faith while dealing with his mother's terminal sickness. And, book-ending the story, a frail, elderly priest recognizes the necessity for a show-down with an old demonic enemy.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=Andrew%20Harmon%20%3Caharmon%40erols.com%3E&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_1"
>Andrew Harmon &lt;aharmon@erols.com&gt;</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Accompanied by her 12 year-old daughter Regan, actress Chris McNeil relocates to Washington D.C. where she is filming a movie. Mother and daughter have a good relationship but after a time Regan begins to act strangely. She undergoes various neurological tests but doctors can find nothing to explain her behavior. As Regan's situation reaches crisis proportions - she has to be tied to her bed, swears like a sailor and speaks in tongues - Chris turns to Father Karras, a Roman Catholic priest and psychiatrist to see if an exorcism might be the solution to their problem. Karras is incredulous but the church eventually agrees calling in Father Merrin, who has previously conducted an exorcism and come face to face with the devil.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=garykmcd&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_2"
>garykmcd</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="odd">
              <p class="plotSummary">
Something beyond evil is happening in a little girl's room. Regan has brutally changed both in the way she looks and the way she acts, with violent outbursts on everyone who comes in contact with her. Her worried mother gets in contact with a priest who comes to the conclusion that Regan is possessed. The top priest who can deal with an exorcism, Father Merrin, is called in to help save Regan from the demon inside her.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=FilmFanUk&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_3"
>FilmFanUk</a></span></em>          </li>
          <li class="even">
              <p class="plotSummary">
A movie actress taking up temporary residence in Washington D.C. has her troubles. The script for the movie she's filming seems inadequate. Her ex, who is also the father of her adolescent daughter, Regan, neglects to call the girl on her birthday. And the attic has rats. Meanwhile, Father Karras, a priest and a psychiatrist, is losing his faith; and he's dealing with a sick mother who needs medical care he hasn't the money to provide. Another priest, the old and ailing Father Merrin, has just returned from Iraq with forebodings of evil. These three persons meet when the sweet and cheerful Regan turns foul-mouthed and violent. But her sickness is beyond the reach of a medical doctor or a psychiatrist. What Regan needs is an exorcist.              </p>
                  <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=J.%20Spurlin&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_4"
>J. Spurlin</a></span></em>          </li>
      <li class="odd">
        <p class="plotSummary">
When a teenage girl is possessed by a mysterious entity, her mother seeks the help of two priests to save her daughter.        </p>
          <span class="nobr">- <em>Written by
<a href="/search/title?plot_author=paulrichard&view=simple&sort=alpha&ref_=ttpl_pl_5"
>paulrichard</a></span></em>      </li>
  </ul>
