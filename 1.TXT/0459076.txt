lonely young man named jack buys a zombie off of a shady home shopping network, he thinks he's found the perfect friend in zach, who despite being quite dead, is the perfect pal. unfortunately for jack, zach becomes increasingly hungry, and it becomes horrifyingly obvious that steak is not on the menu. jack begins to feed his enemies ghoulishly, one by one, to his hungry zombie, as george, his only other friend, begins to carry out a mission to exterminate zach once and for all. gallons of blood begin to flow as jack's enemies rise from their shallow graves to take a ghastly revenge on their killer. as everyone closes in jack must make a choice between the living and the dead, and decide where his friendship lies, while fighting for his life every gore soaked step of the way.        written by  dustin austen all jack wanted was a friend, but he gets more than he bargained for when he orders a zombie off of the internet. when zack begins eating all of jack's friends, jack must
