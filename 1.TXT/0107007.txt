historical and personal events surrounding and including the decisive american civil war battle features thousands of civil war re-enactors marching over the exact ground that the federal army and the army of north virginia fought on. the defense of the little round top and pickett's charge are highlighted in the actual three day battle which is surrounded by the speeches of the commanding officers and the personal reflections of the fighting men. based upon the novel 'the killer angels'.        written by  keith loh  loh@sfu.ca the three day battle that was a turning point in the civil war is shown from the perspectives of both sides, highlighting the fight for little round top, and pickett's charge. other focuses include longstreet(not"longsreet") and lee's relationship as they have differing strategic opinions, armistead fighting on the opposite side of his old friend hancock, and the chamberlain brothers.        written by  anonymous in 1863, the northern and southern forces
