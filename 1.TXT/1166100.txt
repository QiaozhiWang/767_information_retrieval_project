summary showing all 3 plot summaries medical student, sunita, is driven by curiosity to study the case of sanjay singhania, who is afflicted with short-term memory loss. she runs into him, befriends him, and finds out that he is out to kill a seemingly benevolent citizen, ghajini dharmatma. after warning the latter of the impending danger, she subsequently comes across a number of diaries written by sanjay and attempts to put together a jigsaw puzzle as to how a successful and wealthy businessman became a crazed recluse, who re-lives his past through tattoos on his body, notes and polaroid photographs on the wall of his hiranandani complex flat, and his sole obsession of carrying out his deadly mission - little knowing that ghajini and his goons are out to erase every bit of evidence he has gathered and thus ensure that he ends up remembering nothing.        written by  rajoo (gunwanti@hotmail.com) one of the wealthiest business tycoon sanjay singhania (owner of singhania group of industries) who was a very nice businessman and fell in love with a beautiful girl kalpana who was an ad film actress. his life is ruined by a man called ghajini who killed his love and also damaged his brain and two years later he is suffering from a mental disease called short-term memory lost he is living his life by writing notes, clicking pictures and sketching tattoos on his body just to take avenge from the man ghajini.        written by  naved dalkhania a short-term memory loss patient sets out on his journey to avenge the death of his beloved girl.     written by  nebzyl synopsis
