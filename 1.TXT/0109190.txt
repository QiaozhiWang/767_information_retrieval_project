bink couldn't ask for more; he has adoring (if somewhat sickly-sweet) parents, he lives in a huge mansion, and he's just about to appear in the social pages of the paper. unfortunately, not everyone in the world is as nice as baby bink's parents; especially the three enterprising kidnappers who pretend to be photographers from the newspaper. successfully kidnapping baby bink, they have a harder time keeping hold of the rascal, who not only keeps one step ahead of them, but seems to be more than a little bit smarter than the three bumbling criminals.        written by  murray chapman
