showing all 2 plot summaries ron koslow's updated version of the fairytale has a double focus: the relationship between vincent,(a mythic, noble man-beast) and catherine (an asst da in new york); and a secret utopian society of outcasts living in an underground sanctuary where vincent is protected and loved. through an emotional bond connecting vincent to catherine, he comes to be her protector as well as the man she loves. the series follows the developing relationship between them and nicely fleshes out the underground world of labyrinth tunnels, mystical waterfalls, and people who have come together to form a loving and nurturing family.        written by  peg mcnabb  mcnabbp@ctrvax.vanderbilt.edu the adventures and romance of a sensitive and cultured lion-man and a crusading assistant
