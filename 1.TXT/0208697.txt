stripping at a gentlemen's club is a good start. during her first week strutting her stuff on the runway, a wealthy couple pays her to join them at their estate for a scintillating session of voyeuristic sex that leaves gabriella both confused and aroused. but gabriella's real education begins when the man of the house, stephen, makes her his pet project. when he and his exotic servant
