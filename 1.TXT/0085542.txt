this animated tale, a tiny village is destroyed by a surging glacier, which serves as the deadly domain for the evil ice lord, nekron. the only survivor is a young warrior, larn, who vows to avenge this act of destruction. the evil continues, however, as nekron's palace of ice heads straight towards fire keep, the great fortress ruled by the good king jarol. when jarol's beautiful daughter, teegra, is abducted by nekron's sub-human ape-like creatures, larn begins a daring search for her. what results is a tense battle between good and evil, surrounded by the mystical elements of the ancient past.        written by  rone barton lokarr  sandbox2@ix.netcom.com nekron, the evil ice lord uses his powers to conquer the fire keep, the great fortress ruled by the good king jarol. when jarol's beautiful daughter, teegra, is abducted by nekron's sub-human ape-like creatures, her only hope is a young warrior, larn, who begins a daring search for her.        written by  paolo costabel
