secret of the magic scrolls plot summary showing one plot summary wachtell studios and the producer of the amazing adventures of little batman, jordan h. wachtell, presents a new original indiana jones fan short film. in the film, indiana jones and marion (played by adults) are kidnapped by the brother of belloq (the bad guy who melted at the end of raiders) and forced to find magic scrolls that will resurrect the drippy bad guy. in order to get the sacred treasure, however, indy must explore a cursed cave- anyone who enters becomes a little kid again... forever. can indy get the scrolls? does he save marion? what will break the curse?        written by  anonymous synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page" button at the bottom of the page   or learn more
