summaries at the ultra prestigious ouran high school, haruhi fujioka looks for a quiet place to read and walks into an unused music room, and accidentally stumbles across the notorious ouran high school host club, a group of boys who entertain the girls of the school for profit. when haruhi accidentally breaks an expensive vase belonging to the wacky host club, she is made to serve under them until her debt is paid off. haruhi is soon made a host, but in order to pay off what she owes, she must continue to allow the host club's customers to believe she is a boy.        written by  morticon ouran high school host club is about haruhi fujioka, who goes to a school called ouran academy. trying to find a quiet place to study, haruhi meets a group of boys called the ouran high school host club. later on, she gets closer to the members of the host club, and they become the best of friends.        written by  kawaiiemo synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page"
