longshoreman bobo fears he may have killed a man. in his uncertainty, he takes a job on an isolated bait barge. that night, he rescues lovely anna from a watery suicide attempt and installs her on the barge. but tiny, bobo's longtime pal and parasite, hopes to drive anna away before domestic bliss tears bobo away from him; the still unsolved murder may be just the wedge tiny needs. there's fog on
