only thing keeping billy batson from the girl of his dreams is one little condom -- or rather the lack of one. lucky billy finds one at an all-night roadhouse -- but speeding back to his girl, he is killed in a head-on collision with a truck. now invisible, billy must find his sweetheart and rekindle her fire -- but when he comes back to school as a ghost, his first stop is the girl's locker room, and he's beginning to "see" all the possibilities!        written by  concorde - new horizons (with permission). synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page" button at the
