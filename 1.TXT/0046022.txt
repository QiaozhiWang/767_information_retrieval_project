she claims to have lost them. her husband quickly learns of the deceit, which is the beginning of many tragic misunderstandings, all involving the earrings, the general, the countess,  her new lover, the italian baron donati.        written by  gary dickerson  slug@mail.utexas.edu synopsis needing to pay debts comtesse louise de...
