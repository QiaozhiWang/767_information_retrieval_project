summaries in the 13th century there existed a legion of evil knights known as the templars, who quested for eternal life by drinking human blood and committing sacrifices. executed for their unholy deeds, the templars bodies were left out for the crows to peck out their eyes. now, in modern day portugal, a group of people stumble on the templars abandoned monastery, reviving their rotting corpses to terrorize the land.        written by  jeremy lunt  durlinlunt@acadia.net in medieval times, a group of renegade knights were executed for their black magic rituals. in 1970s portugal, vacationing college kids fall prey to the knights' zombified corpses, ressurrected from the dead to feast on the blood of the living.        written by  jojo mac synopsis be the first to contribute!
