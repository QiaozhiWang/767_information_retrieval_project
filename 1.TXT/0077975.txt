faber college has one frat house so disreputable it will take anyone. it has a second one full of white, anglo-saxon, rich young men who are so sanctimonious no one can stand them except dean wormer. the dean enlists the help of the second frat to get the boys of delta house off campus. the dean's plan comes into play just before the homecoming parade to end all parades for all time.        written by  john vogel  jlvogel@comcast.net delta tau chi fraternity at faber college is the bane of dean vernon wormer's existence. it's members don't attend class, are the source of endless pranks on campus and have broken every rule imaginable. as a group, they have a negligible gpa and their main reason for living is to party. when wormer finally finds a way to expel them all, the men of delta tau chi decide to give the college a homecoming parade they will never forget.        written by  garykmcd at a 1962 college, dean vernon wormer is determined to expel the entire delta tau chi fraternity, but those trouble-makers have other plans for him.     written by  kenneth chisholm synopsis in 1962, college freshmen lawrence "larry" kroger (tom hulce) and kent dorfman (stephen furst)
