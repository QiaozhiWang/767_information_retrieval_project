summary this is the end of a glorious military career: general leo fitzjohn retires to his sussex manor where he will write his memoirs. unfortunately, his private life is a disaster: a confirmed womanizer, leo has infuriated his wife emily, now a shrewish and hypochondriac woman, all the more bitter as she still loves him. the general has two plain-looking daughters he dislikes and an attractive french mistress, ghislaine, with whom he has had a platonic affair for seventeen years. when ghislaine resurfaces, determined to complete her love with him and to get rid of emily, leo is at a loss what to do...        written by  guy bellinger synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page" button at the bottom of the page   or learn more in the  synopsis submission
