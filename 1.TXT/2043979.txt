masurian woman, whose husband, a german soldier, was killed in the war, leaving her alone on their farm. a single woman had no defense against russian soldiers who raped as a form of revenge, nor against plundering poles who found themselves in desperate straits. the law of the jungle had replaced the rule of law. help arrives for rose in the form of tadeusz, a former officer in the polish home army who survived the warsaw uprising and is attempting to hide his identity.        written by  tiff synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page" button at the bottom of the
