himself to be the best kung fu fighter in canton. unfortunately, his father, anxious for his son's safety, bribes all his opponents to lose. after a humiliating defeat at the hands of an actor in a traveling theatre company, the son resolves to find a better teacher. furious kung fu battles and slapstick comedy.        written by  richard hills  r.hills@wlv.ac.uk kung fu kung-fu movie about a spoiled youngster, young leong tsan, who hates books and conventions but is obsessed by martial arts. his rich father dotes on him so much that handsome payments are awarded to people who manage to
