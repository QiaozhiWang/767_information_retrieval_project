that it's a good idea to move in together.        written by  amanda lynne b. the brady bunch returns as the kids have grown up and moved out. the series starts with oldest daughters marcia and jan meeting the men of their dreams, falling in love and getting married in a joint ceremony held in their parent's backyard. while some of the other brady offspring make brief appearances, the series
