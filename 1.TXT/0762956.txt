insiders get discovered enhance your imdb page go to imdbpro  help login register login edit reboot the tearing plot summary showing all 2 plot summaries written by  rob can megabyte asks bob for a favor, but bob isn't interested in helping a virus. because of that megabyte sends his viral binomes to trash dot's diner. and will continue to threaten his friends unless he does the favor. dot, enzo, and the other binomes told bob not to trust megabyte at all. but when a game cube is being input by the user, the viral binomes prevent bob from entering the game, and is unable to play it once it hits the surface of mainframe. unfortunatly the user won the game and those who were in the game are nullified. this leads bob no choice and went to do megabyte's favor. his favor turns out to be turning a tear into a portal to the super computer. but is bob going to help a virus make his way in infecting the super computer with an army of viral binomes?        written by  j legault synopsis
