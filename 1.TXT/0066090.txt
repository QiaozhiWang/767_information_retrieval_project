       written by  john oswalt  jao@jao.com a terrorist group known as the molly maguires has been sabotaging a coal mine in pennsylvania where they in fact work. detective mcparlan as mckenna must infiltrate the group led by john kehoe. throughout the film mcparlan's allegiances are tested. will he side with the maguires and become a true member, or keep his mission in mind and bring an end to the group?
