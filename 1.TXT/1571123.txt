summaries mike's discovery of a dinosaur bone in the quarry causes the plant to get shut down for scientific research - leaving him out of a job. but he begins to feel like a dinosaur himself when he discovers how drastically things have changed technologically over the years in terms of searching for a job. meanwhile, frankie feels she must once again come to brick's rescue when he decides to run for school historian.        written by  abc publicity mike proudly poses with a dinosaur bone found in his quarry, only to have his whole team laid off until the site has been archaeologically cleared. mike now learns the old-fashioned job hunt has become a scam, axl's relaxed virtual approach does work, however often he looses his jobs. brick runs for school historian but neglects campaigning as sue often did (to no avail), so frankie steps in, only to find brick swings it with a cleverly pragmatic nerd-speech in assembly.        written by  kgf vissers mike is forced to look for a job when the quarry temporarily shuts down, and frankie is forced to do all the work as brick runs for class historian.     written by  jgp3553@yahoo.com synopsis be the first to contribute!
