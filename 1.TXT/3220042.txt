2 plot summaries happy potter is the first fan film in the harry potter fan films based on the movies ''harry potter.'' it is the tale of happy potter, an ordinary 18-year-old boy serving as a sort of slave for his aunt and uncle who learns that he is actually a wizard and has been invited to attend the ucla school for witchcraft and wizardry. happy is snatched away from his mundane existence by hagrid, the grounds keeper for ucla, and quickly thrown into a world completely foreign to both him and the viewer. famous for an incident that happened at his birth, happy makes friends easily at his new school class. he soon finds, however, that the wizard world is far more dangerous for him than he would have imagined, and he quickly learns that not all wizards are ones to be trusted.
