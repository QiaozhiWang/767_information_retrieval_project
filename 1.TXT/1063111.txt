her daughter and their future may not be what's best for him. tommy carson works around the clock to keep the peace in prospect, but donny, who is on a downward spiral, makes this even more difficult for the deputy. and les just wants to fit in with his big brother's friends, but his overbearing mother desperately wants him to stand out. the subtle interconnections of these character's lives will
