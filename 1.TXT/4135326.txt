coming of age on a northern california commune in the 1970s. while lane enjoys the freedom of living off-the-grid with her mother and younger siblings, she craves a stable "normal" life - a life she's only seen in pictures from a stolen sears catalog. lane must navigate her troubled mother, hallelujah, while trying to care for her younger brother and sister. after a series of dangerous and isolating events, lane decides to forge her own path in hopes of finding a better life after it becomes clear her family has fallen apart.        written by  jw synopsis be the first to contribute!
