hoyle, a girl with a sharp mind and a weakness for bourbon, finds herself on the trail of a reclusive genius. but her work takes a series of unforeseen twists as events around her grow increasingly fragmented... disconnected... surreal. with an ethereal lounge singer and her loyal partner as her only allies, hoyle is plunged into a dark world of intrigue and earth-shattering cosmological secrets. haunted by an ever-present shadow whom she is destined to face, hoyle discovers that the most powerful force in the universe -- the power to bend reality, the power to know the truth -- lies
