blonde actress is preparing for her biggest role yet, but when she finds herself falling for her co-star, she realizes that her life is beginning to mimic the fictional film that they're shooting. adding to her confusion is the revelation that the current film is a remake of a doomed polish production, 47, which was never finished due to an unspeakable tragedy.        written by  ted after taking the lead in a new movie hollywood star nikki grace learns the script is based on an old polish film, which was abandoned after the two lead roles of the film were murdered, thinking the film is cursed nikki's imagination runs riot.        written by  ted the actress nikki grace is invited to perform the role of susan blue as the lead actress of the movie "on high in blue tomorrows", directed by kingsley stewart. nikki's husband is a jealous guy and the co-star is the wolf devon berk that will perform billy side, the husband of susan. in the rehearsal, kingsley tells that the movie is a remake of an unfinished and damned polish production where the two lead stars had been murdered. while shooting the movie, nikki has daydreams, mixing her real life with the fictional susan.        written by  claudio carvalho, rio de janeiro, brazil as an actress starts to adopt the persona of her character in a film, her world starts to become nightmarish and surreal.     written by  anton m.
