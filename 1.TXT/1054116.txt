1979 is ending, the 80s are approaching rapidly. carl and robert, two slacker best friends who smoke hashish as a way of cheerful living - have been peddling hashish for a decade, hitting an all time high with this last batch: 45 kilos of pure, high grade nepalese hashish destined for the draught-dry oslo streets. in their own eyes they are not criminals they simply provide the life-essential drug to a suffering population. but then glenn comes along, their friend and ambitious financial-backer-destined-to-be-a-yuppie. he has plans to make this deal even bigger than they have been led to believe. following the delivery of the hashish, the police are on their tail for heroin-smuggling a fact they cannot explain as they both hate heroin and would never touch the drug. finally they both awake from their self-induced haze, and fight back in a hilarious, suspenseful and dramatic ending.        written by  mondo synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page" button at
