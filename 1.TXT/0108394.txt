'blue' is the story of julie who loses her husband, an acclaimed composer and her young daughter in a car accident. the film's theme of liberty is manifested in julie's attempt to start life anew, free of personal commitments, belongings, grief or love. she intends to numb herself by withdrawing from the world and living completely independently, anonymously and in solitude in the parisian metropolis. despite her intentions, people from her former and present life intrude with their own needs. however, the reality created by the people who need and care about her, a surprising discovery and the music around which the film revolves heal julie and draws her back to the land of the living.        written by  anonymous a woman struggles to find a way to live her life after the death of her husband and child.     written by  murray chapman  muzzle@imdb.com and brian mcinnis synopsis julie, wife of the famous composer patrice de courcy, must cope with the sudden death of her husband and daughter in an automobile accident she herself survives...   see full synopsis see also taglines synopsis plot keywords parents guide getting started contributor zone contribute to this page edit page three colors: blue storyline taglines plot summary synopsis plot keywords parents guide details full cast and crew release dates official sites box office/business company credits filming locations technical specs literature did you know?
