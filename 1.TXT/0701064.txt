friend falls in love plot summary showing all 2 plot summaries a new girl in class samantha stanky comes between bart and milhouse when milhouse falls for her and starts to ignore bart. bart snitches on the couple to samantha's father, who has her sent to a convent school, where milhouse and bart visit and she confesses to still having feelings for milhouse. the boys get back together again whilst homer increases his word power after receiving a vocabulary building subliminal tape and not the weight loss tape expected.        written by  don @ minifie-1 milhouse falls in love with samantha, a new transfer student, and bart becomes jealous of all the time they are spending together. meanwhile, homer orders a subliminal weight loss tape, but is given a vocabulary builder by mistake.
