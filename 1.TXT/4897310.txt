plot summary showing one plot summary launched in 1992 as a companion for "beverly hills, 90210," "melrose place" quickly became a giant television hit in its own right. originally designed to be an authentic, grounded look at 20-something life in los angeles, the program soon became "90210"'s soapier, yet equally popular counterpart. the 'unauthorized melrose place story' highlights the remarkable behind-the-scenes moments that shaped the series including cast drama, heather locklear arriving to "save" the show, and the actors begging their writers for even wilder story lines to make
