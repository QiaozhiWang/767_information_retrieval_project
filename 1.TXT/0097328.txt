jessie is an aging career criminal who has been in more jails, fights, schemes, and lineups than just about anyone else. his son vito, while currently on the straight and narrow, has had a fairly shady past and is indeed no stranger to illegal activity. they both have great hope for adam, vito's son and jessie's grandson, who is bright, good-looking, and without a criminal past. so when adam approaches jessie with a scheme for a burglary he's shocked, but not necessarily uninterested.        written by  murray chapman  muzzle@cs.uq.oz.au synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the
