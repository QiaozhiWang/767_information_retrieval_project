their son hank are on a touring holiday of africa when they meet the mysterious louis bernard on a bus. the next day bernard is murdered in the local marketplace, but before he dies he manages to restaurant, and is due to take place in london in a few days' time. in order to prevent mckenna from going to the police, the couple kidnap hank. ben is warned not to tell the police what he knows.
