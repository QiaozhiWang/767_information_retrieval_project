showing one plot summary professor gromov constructs a robot called electronic, which looks exactly like sergey syroezhkibn, a 6-grader from one of odessa (ussr) schools. the robot also acts a lot like a human, and its dream is to become a real man. electronic escapes from the professor's lab and accidentally meets sergey, his prototype. meanwhile, a gang lead by stump is trying to kidnap electronic to make him steal pictures from museums. for this purpose they send their hitman urrie.        written by  pavel volkov  xenofex@newmail.ru synopsis be the first to contribute! just click
