hollywood insiders get discovered enhance your imdb page go to imdbpro  help login register login edit funny farm plot summary showing all 2 plot summaries when andy and elizabeth buy a farm in vermont, they can't imagine the trouble that awaits them. andy has quit his job as a sports journalist and is planning to use the peace and quiet of the country to write the great american novel. from the moment the movers' truck gets lost with their furniture, though, there's little peace and less quiet. from a manical mailman to a dead body buried in the garden, andy is distracted by the town and its wacky inhabitants. his effort at a novel is mediocre, at best, and he's threatened by elizabeth's foray into writing when she attempts a children's book. can the farmers survive the townsfolk and each other?        written by  rick munoz  rick.munoz@his.com andy and elizabeth are sick of life in the city, and decide to move to the country. buying a home near a picturesque town, then soon discover (to their horror) that things are done differently in the country. they must deal with all of the local characters, the local animals, as well as any skeletons in the closet.        written
