summary showing all 2 plot summaries klaus and damon use jeremy's posthumous witches dimension to draw connor's magically invisible tattoo. while a hybrid guards the slayer, klaus explains to the gang that it belongs to the five, the ultimate slayers brotherhood, which nearly wiped out the originals in 11th century italy, after rebekah fell in love with member alexander. the brothers agree to team up and trick rebekah, who knows where the key to the five's ultimate weapon is, an engraved sword. meanwhile damon escorts elena, whom only stefano can convince life as vampire is still worth living, to a fraternity party for a blood hunt lesson, and to the witchcraft course of professor atticus shane, ignoring he's a mortal enemy.        written by  kgf vissers at a fraternity costume party, damon gives elena a lesson in choosing a victim. klaus reveals an encounter with a group of vampire hunters - flashing back to 12th century italy.     written by  anonymous synopsis 1110 a.d. a witch recites an incantation in the forest and when she's finished, she sets down her cauldron of flame and lets the spell travel up the arms and onto the bodies of five (shockingly hairless for the time
