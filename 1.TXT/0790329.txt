treasure of sara madre plot summary showing all 2 plot summaries an in memoriam for fallen teacher sara madre (long deceased) turns into dreams of avarice when an accident reveals a map leading to her hidden wealth. being that she was a dancer, the map indicates the location via a series of dance moves. bobby sells copies, reaping substantial benefits as friend sets upon friend, all seeking to discover treasure. unfortunately, it's all a scam set up by bobby, but when sara's hat turns up among the kids' unearthed trash, he believes they've come close to actually finding her missing treasure. as he and cohort donkeylips take over the hunt, two-stepping becomes two-timing as neither partner trusts the other. meanwhile, impending photographs for a new camp brochure has ug focused entirely on grooming the camp to perfection.        written by  statmanjeff dance fever hits camp when a treasure map turns up bearing directions to a fortune written up as a series of dance moves.
