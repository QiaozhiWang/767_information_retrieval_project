peter sellers plays aldo vanucci (aka the fox), one of the greatest criminals of the world, and master of disguise. after aldo escapes from the italian prison he was held in, he meets again with his friends, and plans to retrieve the "gold of cairo" a large shipment of gold, that waits to be unloaded somewhere in italy. aldo devices the perfect plan. posing as a famous director, he finds the ideal coastal village to unload the shipment, and persuades the entire population that he has chosen their village as the set for his new movie. everybody, including the idiot chief of the local police is so excited, that they can't even imagine that in fact they are helping the fox to get the "gold of cairo"...        written by  chris makrozahopoulos  makzax@hotmail.com the fox is a criminal mastermind vacationing in one of italy's finest prisons sending care packages home with each visitor. when he finds that his little sister has begun to discover boys and that his dim witted men don't think they can handle her, he vows to escape. after he eludes the manhunt, he accepts a job to smuggle gold into the country for which he assembles an entire movie company, including an aging star (victor mature) for cover as he manages to stay several steps ahead of the authorities. little sister, in her own way manages to keep up with him nicely.        written by  john vogel  jlvogel@comcast.net synopsis released in the us as "after the fox", caccia alla volpe is a 1966 collaboration of vittorio de sica and peter sellers...   see full synopsis see also taglines synopsis
