destruction plot summary showing all 2 plot summaries north korea seems to be opening up to the outside world under the rule of choi kim. however, his son, choi song, a top commander of the north korean army, leads a coup that has the leaders of north and south korea killed. north korea descends into isolation, and it is revealed that it has exported nuclear material to outside terrorist groups. in retaliation, north korea is overrun by outside forces and song goes into hiding. but the threat is not yet over; song has access to nuclear missiles and threatens to use them on the outside world. each faction in the country has its own agenda: china seeks to annex north korea, and south korea has the same goal in mind. the allied nations (a parody of the united nations) seeks to restore peace, while the russian mafia seeks to profit from the instability. all four factions are fighting the north korean military. the player must work with each faction in order to find song.        written by  sam korea has become a war zone when the president is assumed dead and song, a ruthless dictator, takes over. suddenly, the allied nations, china, the russian mafia, and south korea are all engaged in this war, and all hating each other. in order to attempt to restore peace, the allies send in a mercenary who can work with all 4 factions to take down the deck of 52, the codename for song's 51 generals, along with song himself as the ace of spades. what's in it for the mercenary? a $100,000,000 bounty on song if taken alive. time to load your gun and join the fight.        written
