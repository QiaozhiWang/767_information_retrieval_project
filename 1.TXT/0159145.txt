o-benkyô yarô plot summary showing one plot summary golden boy is the story of kintaro oe, a twenty-five-year-old in pursuit of life's lessons. a brilliant student, he dropped out of tokyo universiy's law program because he had had mastered the entire curriculum. now he lives a simple life, traveling on his bicycle and working numerous part-time jobs ranging from software companies to noodle restaurants in order to learn everything that he can about the world. although he's a kind and giving person, kintaro's only weakness lies in his lust for beautiful women. however, as long as he can control himself, he may just manage to save the day, and win a few hearts in the process.        written by  anonymous synopsis be the first to contribute! just click the "edit page" button at the
