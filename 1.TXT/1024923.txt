summary showing all 4 plot summaries this entry in the star wars saga casts players as darth vader's "secret apprentice", unveiling new revelations about the star wars galaxy. the expansive story, created under direction from george lucas, is set during the largely unexplored era between star wars: episode iii revenge of the sith and star wars: episode iv a new hope. in it, players will assist the iconic villain in his quest to rid the universe of jedi - and face decisions that could change the course of their destiny. as its name implies, the force unleashed completely re-imagines the scope and scale of the force by taking full advantage of newly developed technologies only possible on next-gen consoles that will be seen and experienced for the first time anywhere in videogaming.
