girlhood in the roaring twenties. in flashback, margie, a smarter, less popular girl at central high, meets handsome new french teacher ralph fontayne; circumstances keep throwing them together and margie, in company with every other girl in school, develops a crush on him. then margie's date for the prom gets sick, and what happens next surprises everyone.        written by  rod crawford
