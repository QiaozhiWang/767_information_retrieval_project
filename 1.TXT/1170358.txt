dangerous journey, they need to fight against giant spiders and orcs and they are captured but flee from the elves. but smaug is not easily deceived and flies to lake-town to destroy the town and the dwellers.        written by  claudio carvalho, rio de janeiro, brazil the dwarves, along with bilbo baggins and gandalf the grey, continue their quest to reclaim erebor, their homeland, from smaug.
