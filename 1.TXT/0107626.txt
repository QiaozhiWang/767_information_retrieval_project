missy works gets robbed and johnny is killed trying to help. but johnny doesn't want to stay dead, so he returns to his life like nothing ever happened. after a while he discovers that his body is disintegrating and he must feed with human flesh if he wants to survive. looks like he is a real zombie...        written by  chris makrozahopoulos  makzax@hotmail.com synopsis be the first to
