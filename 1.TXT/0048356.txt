and he faces permanent bachelorhood. but when he is goaded by his mother into going to the stardust ballroom one saturday night, marty unexpectedly meets clara, a lonely teacher. suddenly, marty's future seems bright. winner of best picture of 1955, best adapted screenplay for  paddy chayefsky delbert mann ernest borgnine written by  trivwhiz a middle-aged butcher and a school teacher who have
