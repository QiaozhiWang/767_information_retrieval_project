showing one plot summary gadget (again voiced by maurice lamarche) is now a lieutenant, and a member of an organization called "womp" (world organization of mega powers). he is aided in his work by penny (who is now twelve years old), as well as the new robot characters digit and fidget, the titular gadgetinis invented by penny. brain and chief quimby make brief appearances in the series as well. it follows the same plot as the original series, with the clumsy gadget attempting to fight crime on his own, while penny and her helpers do all the work. doctor claw, now with the first name george, returns as the main villain, with his relatives introduced in some episodes. in some stories, he is replaced by other villains.        written by  masked man synopsis be the first to
