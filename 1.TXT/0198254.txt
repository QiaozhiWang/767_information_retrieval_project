showing one plot summary an updated version of the classic tom  jerry cartoons from 1940s/ 1950s. the 'kids' in these cartoons are far less violent than their parents were, but still find ways to cause plenty of trouble for each other and everyone else around them. the show also features other tex avery characters such as droopy and spike, along with their kids.        written by  jean-marc
