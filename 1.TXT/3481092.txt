summary showing all 2 plot summaries during flamboyant buddy zach's vacation, andrew enjoyed teaming up with considerate george. when zach realizes his quirks diminish andrew's eagerness to be his wingman, his semi-jocular suggestion to try other partners is taken seriously, but although it works professionally for andrew, both friends are miserable without their mate. a campaign for baby animal suits arouses simon's hope of grandchildren, from sydney, whose sexually enjoyable relationship with laid-back neighbor hunk owen is under increasing stress as she tries to hide and deny the truth.        written by  kgf vissers simon's desperation for grandchildren forces him to interfere with sydney's new relationship. meanwhile, zach and andrew try working with other partners.
