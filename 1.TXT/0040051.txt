showing one plot summary a pinnacle of the golden age of television, "studio one" presented a wide range of memorable dramas and received 18-emmy nominations and five wins during its prestigious nine-year-run on cbs. showcasing some of the greatest talents of the era, this groundbreaking series created an enormous impact and still remains a treasured part of america's broadcasting history. embracing the work of some of television's most iconic writers, directors, actors and technical artists, the studio one anthology features the complete 1954 original television production of "twelve angry men" and is highlighted by early performances by charlton heston, art carney, jack lemmon and leslie nielsen as well as teleplays written by rod serling and gore vidal.        synopsis be the
