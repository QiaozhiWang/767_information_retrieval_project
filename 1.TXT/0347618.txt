a young girl rescues a mysterious cat from traffic and soon finds herself the unwelcome recipient of gifts and favors from the king of the cats, who also wants her to marry his son, prince lune. with the assistance of a fat, grouchy real cat and a an elegant cat statuette come to life (both characters featured in studio ghibli's earlier anime "whisper of the heart"), the girl visits the cat kingdom and narrowly escapes again.        written by  castipiani after helping a cat, a young girl finds herself involuntarily engaged to a cat prince in a magical world where her only hope of freedom lies with a dapper cat statuette come to life.     written by  kenneth chisholm synopsis a young girl named haru awakens to another day, where it seems she's a few steps behind everything.
