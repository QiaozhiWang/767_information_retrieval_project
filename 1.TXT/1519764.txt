showing one plot summary mob baron burrelli asks fbi's help to solve the theft of his parish church's bible, from 17th century naples, where it would have worked several miracles. neal assumes it may be meant for healing, as it's very hard to fence, having contacted expert fiametta, who points out it's actually a breviary, not a bible. neal and peter soon find the vagrant who was hired to steal it but robbed himself, and suspect a link with the nearby murder of rivaling mob family member, which is alas assigned exclusive to a jealously rivaling fbi agent from organized crime.        written by
