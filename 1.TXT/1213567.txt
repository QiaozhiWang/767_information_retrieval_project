indiana jones: demons of deception plot summary showing one plot summary september, 1916. as corporal henri defense, young indiana jones has become a motorcycle courier stationed near the trenches at verdun. his friend remy is still in the trenches, and both grow more disillusioned about the war each day. chosen to sneak into enemy territory on account of his gift for languages, indy overhears german officers speaking of the imminent arrival of their most powerful artillery guns, nicknamed big bertha. a month later indy and remy are granted leave to paris, where indiana meets the exotic dancer mata hari at a dinner party. he falls for her immediately, but soon finds out she is dating other men as well, including the french minister of war.        written by  il tesoro synopsis be the
